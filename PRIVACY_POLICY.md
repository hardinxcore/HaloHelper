# Privacy Policy for Halo Helper

The **Halo Helper** browser extension respects your privacy. It handles your data completely locally within your browser and does not track you, collect metrics, or sell any of your information to third parties.

## Data Collection
We do not collect any personal data. "Halo Helper" operates solely as a local interface improvement tool for your browser. It does not "phone home", nor does it contain analytics, advertisements, or tracking codes.

## Storage and API Access
- **Settings & History:** Any configuration you set (such as your specific Halo domain) and your recent ticket history are saved strictly locally using your browser's local storage API. This data never leaves your device.
- **API Authentication:** The background service uses your active session cookies only to securely read data from your own defined HaloPSA or HaloITSM API endpoints. No login credentials or API keys are ever stored within the extension, and the generated authorization tokens are strictly used to communicate with the application's native domain.

## Contact
If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the extension's code, please feel free to create an Issue directly in the related GitHub repository.
